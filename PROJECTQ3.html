Fire accidents can happen anywhere, and anytime, so knowing how to properly prevent one from happening is a crucial skill! In the steps below, I will be outlining how to lower the likelihood of a fire from happening.

How to Prevent Fire Accidents at Home
One of the #1 ways to prevent any fire accidents is unplugging unused appliances. This is one of the easiest ways that you can easily implement into your daily routine, and can also benefit the environment since it lowers electricity usage! (https://www.gettyimages.com.au/photos/electrical-plug-socket)
Use caution with open flames. When cooking food, treat fire with caution, as it can spread easily. (https://www.deviantart.com/faerykisses/art/Fire-stock-6978590)
Don't charge your electronics overnight. This is one of the leading causes of house fire accidents. By fixing this bad habit, it can drastically lower the likelihood of a fire accident.
Keep flammable objects such as lighter (fluid) away from children. Children can cause many problems (I know this myself as I am a child), especially in relation to accidents.
